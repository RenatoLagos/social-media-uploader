Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

This application collects only the information necessary to upload videos:

  • OAuth tokens for platform authentication
  • Video files you choose to upload

2. How We Use Information

  • OAuth tokens: Used solely to authenticate with social media platforms
  • Video files: Processed locally and uploaded to your chosen platforms

3. Data Storage

  • All credentials are stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to third-party servers except the social media platforms you authorize

4. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • TikTok (via TikTok API)
  • Instagram (via Instagram API)
  • YouTube (via YouTube Data API)
  • OpenAI (for generating descriptions)

Each service has its own privacy policy.

5. Data Security

Credentials are stored locally in your device's file system. You are responsible for securing your device.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time through each platform's settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time.
